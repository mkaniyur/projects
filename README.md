# Projects

## newsSentiments

Sentiment analysis & visualization of news headlines from 2 major newspapers, the Wall Street Journal and New York Times, over the course of 2020-2025. Coded in Python.

## redBlackTree

Implementation of a red-black tree. Coded in Python.

## lispSimulators

Implementation of a register machine language simulator in Lisp & implementation of a Lisp simulator on top of the RML simulator. An additional version of the Lisp simulator with a UI. Coded in Scheme Lisp.

## mobilityTrajectories

Application & testing of a spatial clustering method to predict individuals' employment and residence locations using mobility data & a limited ground-truth dataset.
